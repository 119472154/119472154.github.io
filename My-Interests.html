My Interests

Sport:
Soccer is my number one favourite sport and has been all my life. I have represented my county, my province, and my country at various levels. It has been my dream for so long to become a professional and I dont think it will ever stop. I am also quite a fan of golf. In fact, I competed in a number of tournaments in the past and even earned cash prizes. One of my uncles was a pro and so that's how it began for me. I usually play throughout the entirety of the summer. Skking is one that came about recently. I went whilst in secondary school with a group of about 50 and ever since i have been obsessed. I am already planning on when to go next.
Travel:
Travelling is something that I am very passionate about. Being from South Africa I was born into a family that just loved to travel. I have been to 4 continents and I spend all the money I receive on exploring new places.
Hobbies:
I spend most of my spare time with friends and family. I am a peoples' person and find it difficult to entertain myself alone. Usually I like to either play online games, watch sport or go around to a friends house. Recently however, I passed my Driving Test and now my friends and I can go wherever,whenever.
